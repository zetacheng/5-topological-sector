# Claim Ledger

Allowed statuses: `PROPOSED`, `SUPPORTED`, `VERIFIED`, `FAILED`, `RETIRED`, `CONDITIONAL`, `INCONCLUSIVE`.

`VERIFIED` requires a closed gate, reproducible artifacts, and reviewer acceptance. `SUPPORTED` is weaker than `VERIFIED`. Failed and retired claims must remain recorded for provenance and must not be deleted.
